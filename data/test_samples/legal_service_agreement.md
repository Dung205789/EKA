# Service Agreement (Sample)

## 1. Parties
This Service Agreement is entered into between Alpha Solutions LLC ("Provider") and Bright Retail JSC ("Client").

## 2. Term
The term starts on 2026-01-01 and ends on 2026-12-31 unless terminated earlier under Section 8.

## 3. Fees and Payment
Client shall pay a monthly service fee of 45,000,000 VND.
Invoices are due within 15 days of receipt.
Late payments incur interest at 1.0% per month.

## 4. Service Levels
Provider commits to 99.9% monthly uptime.
For any month with uptime below 99.0%, Client receives a service credit of 10% of monthly fees.

## 5. Confidentiality
Each party must protect confidential information using reasonable safeguards and may use it only for purposes of this Agreement.

## 6. Data Protection
Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect Client data.

## 7. Limitation of Liability
Except for gross negligence, fraud, or confidentiality breaches, each party's total liability is limited to fees paid by Client in the prior 6 months.

## 8. Termination
Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach is not cured within 30 days after written notice.

## 9. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of Singapore.

## 10. Dispute Resolution
Disputes shall first be negotiated in good faith for 30 days, then submitted to arbitration in Singapore under SIAC Rules.
