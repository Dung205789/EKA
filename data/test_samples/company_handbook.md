# Company Handbook 2026

## Work Hours
- Core hours: 09:00-16:00 (Mon-Fri)
- Flexible start: 07:30-10:00
- Standard work week: 40 hours

## Leave Policy
- Annual leave: 12 days/year
- Sick leave: 6 days/year
- Carry-over: max 5 days to next year

## Expense Policy
- Meals: up to 250,000 VND/day when traveling
- Hotel: up to 1,200,000 VND/night
- Taxi: reimbursable with receipt

## IT Security
- Password must be at least 12 characters
- Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for admin accounts
- Lost laptop must be reported within 2 hours

## Remote Work
- Maximum remote days: 3 days/week
- Team lead approval required for fully remote arrangements
